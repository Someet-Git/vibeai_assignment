## Usage

Simply type your message and press Enter or click Send. The AI will respond with empathetic, supportive messages.

### Example Prompts

- "I'm feeling overwhelmed with work lately"
- "My friend hasn't talked to me in weeks"
- "I just got some exciting news!"
- "I've been feeling lonely since moving"

## Limitations

- This is an AI companion, **not a replacement for professional mental health support**
- If you're in crisis, please reach out to a [helpline](https://findahelpline.com/)
- The model may occasionally generate responses that don't perfectly match the context
